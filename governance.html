Governance Gaps Often Stay Invisible — Until They Surface

Governance risks don’t always appear immediately. They surface during audits, inspections, disputes, funding reviews, or operational transitions.

What’s Actually at Stake?

Financial Exposure

  • Penalties for delayed statutory filings

  • Interest on missed PF/ESIC payments

  • Backdated compliance costs

  • Regulatory notices & compliance escalations

Operational Disruption

  • Payroll inconsistencies

  • Incorrect salary structuring

  • Unclear employment terms

  • Founder-dependent processes

Legal & Regulatory Risk

  • Missing registrations

  • No POSH framework

  • Improper documentation

  • Employee misclassification

Growth & Funding Impact

  • Red flags during investor duligence

  • Delays in expansion across states

  • Compliance gaps affecting valuation

  • Reduced credibility with institutional stakeholders

Quick Governance Self-Assessment

  • Do we have all mandatory registrations completed?
  • Is our salary structure fully statutory compliant?
  • Do we maintain a structured compliance calendar?
  • Do we have documented HR policies and appointment templates?
  • Is POSH compliance formally in place?

If you're unsure about even one — your governance foundation likely needs attention.
