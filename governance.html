Governance Gaps Often Stay Invisible — Until They Surface

Governance risks don’t always appear immediately. They surface during audits, inspections, disputes, funding reviews, or operational transitions.

Financial Exposure

  • Penalties for delayed statutory filings
  • Interest on missed PF / ESIC payments
  • Backdated compliance costs

Operational Disruption

  • Payroll inconsistencies
  • Unclear employment terms
  • Founder-dependent processes

Legal & Regulatory Risk

  • Missing registrations
  • Improper documentation
  • Undefined internal controls

Growth & Due Diligence Impact

  • Red flags during investor reviews
  • Compliance gaps affecting expansion
  • Governance inconsistencies impacting valuation

Governance Self-Assessment

Is Your Governance Foundation Fully Structured?

  • All statutory registrations completed and updated
  • Payroll fully aligned with statutory deductions
  • Documented HR policies & appointment templates
  • Formal POSH compliance framework
  • Structured compliance calendar with tracking
  • Defined internal control & documentation ownership

If you are uncertain about any of the above, your governance framework may require structured review.
