The Risk of Informal Growth

As organizations scale, informal systems begin to break. Decisions decentralize, compliance complexity increases, and undocumented processes turn into structural exposure.

  • Regulatory gaps multiply silently
  • Founder dependency increases operational risk
  • Documentation inconsistency creates legal vulnerability
  • Audit readiness becomes reactive instead of proactive
